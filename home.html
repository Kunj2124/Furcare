Got questions about FurCare?
No Worries, We've got you covered!

We've put together some frequently asked questions to help you better understand our mission, features, and how you can get involved in making a difference for street dogs.

FurCare is a platform dedicated to rescuing, treating, and rehoming street dogs. We connect users with nearby vets, volunteers, and NGOs to assist in animal care. You can donate, volunteer, or adopt dogs through the platform, while earning rewards for your contributions.

You can use our "Pet Matchmaking" feature, which connects you with dogs available for adoption based on your preferences. Simply browse, choose a dog, and we’ll guide you through the adoption process with the nearest shelters or rescues.

FurCare offers volunteering opportunities for individuals who want to assist with rescues or routine vet checkups for street dogs. You can sign up for local events, join rescue missions, or help by spreading awareness in your community.

You can donate money, food, accessories, or even medical supplies to help street dogs. Our donation options also allow you to sponsor specific dogs or contribute to larger rescue efforts.

Our AI assistant provides instant guidance for basic treatments, and we help connect you with nearby vets or volunteers to provide professional care. You can also report injured dogs through the platform, and we’ll coordinate rescue efforts.

Services We Offer

Users can browse profiles of adoptable animals, view their details, and communicate with shelters. We offer resources for responsible pet ownership to ensure a smooth adoption process.

This convenient and reliable service significantly reduces stress for both pets and their owners, ensuring that every pet receives quality care in the comfort of a familiar environment that they know and love.

This service empowers individuals to assist stray dogs in their area by connecting them with our organization, ensuring a loving home for each pup.

This service ensures that all pets receive essential health evaluations, vaccinations, and preventative care, promoting overall well-being and supporting responsible pet ownership and care for strays.

Users can ask questions and receive personalized advice and tips, enhancing their pet ownership experience and helping them make informed decisions for their furry friends.

Users can input their location to receive a list of nearby vets, complete with contact details and services offered. This tool helps pet owners quickly find trusted care, enhancing access to essential veterinary services.
